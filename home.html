How does the corona virus spread?

The virus causes mild symptoms including a cough, fever, and shortness of breath in most people who get it. However, some cases can lead to severe illness. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), roughly 80% of those who contract the virus will not need special treatment and will recover by themselves. However, 1 in 6 people can become seriously ill.
